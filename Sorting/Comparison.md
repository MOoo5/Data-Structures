## Comparison Table

| Algorithm | Best | Average | Worst | Space | Stable? | When to Use? |
|-----------|------|---------|-------|-------|---------|--------------|
| **Bubble** | O(n) | O(n²) | O(n²) | O(1) | ✅ | Small arrays, educational |
| **Selection** | O(n²) | O(n²) | O(n²) | O(1) | ❌ | Small arrays, memory limited |
| **Insertion** | O(n) | O(n²) | O(n²) | O(1) | ✅ | Nearly sorted, small arrays |
| **Merge** | O(n log n) | O(n log n) | O(n log n) | O(n) | ✅ | Large arrays, stable needed |
| **Quick** | O(n log n) | O(n log n) | O(n²) | O(log n) | ❌ | Large arrays, average case |
